Abstract

This paper proposes a retrieval-augmented generation (RAG) framework that fuses information retrieval with generative modeling to create contextually grounded AI outputs. By coupling search-driven precision with generative adaptability, the approach improves factual consistency, explainability, and multi-domain performance across enterprise use cases.
