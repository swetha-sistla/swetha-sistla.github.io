Abstract

This article delves into the evolution of Named Entity Recognition (NER) from classical rule-based systems to transformer-based deep models. It compares contextual embeddings, transfer learning, and attention mechanisms that enhance entity extraction accuracy in financial, biomedical, and multilingual domains.
