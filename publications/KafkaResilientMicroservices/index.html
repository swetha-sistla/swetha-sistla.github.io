Abstract

This paper explores the use of Apache Kafka as a backbone for building resilient and scalable microservice architectures. It presents event-driven communication models, consumer group strategies, and error-handling patterns that ensure high availability and eventual consistency. The study also discusses performance optimization and monitoring techniques for distributed financial systems.
