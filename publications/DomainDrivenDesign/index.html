Abstract

This article explores how Domain-Driven Design (DDD) enables architectural clarity and business alignment in modern software ecosystems. It discusses bounded contexts, aggregate patterns, and tactical modeling techniques to decompose monoliths into maintainable, scalable microservices, ensuring long-term agility and operational excellence.
