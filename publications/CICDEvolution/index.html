Abstract

This article provides a comprehensive view of the evolution of Continuous Integration and Continuous Delivery (CI/CD), from early automated builds to modern AI-driven DevOps pipelines. It examines workflow orchestration, testing automation, containerization, and the cultural shifts enabling faster and more reliable software releases.
