Abstract

This paper explores how Robotic Process Automation (RPA) revolutionizes financial software ecosystems by automating repetitive workflows, reducing operational costs, and improving accuracy in financial reporting. It highlights frameworks for implementation, key metrics for ROI evaluation, and governance strategies to ensure compliance and sustainability in large-scale finance automation programs.
