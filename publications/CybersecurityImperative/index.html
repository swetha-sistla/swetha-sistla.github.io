Abstract

This paper explores the accelerating pace of cybersecurity threats in the digital era, emphasizing AI-enhanced intrusion detection, cloud-native security architectures, and resilience engineering principles. It provides strategic insights into zero-trust adoption, proactive threat hunting, and organizational frameworks that balance innovation with defense.
