Abstract

This paper delves into the methodologies and cognitive frameworks behind effective prompt engineering. It outlines prompt design patterns, context injection strategies, few-shot and chain-of-thought prompting, and introduces evaluation metrics for reliability, interpretability, and ethical prompt outcomes in generative AI systems.
