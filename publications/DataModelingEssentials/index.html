Abstract

This paper presents a detailed overview of data modeling principles and their evolution across modern architectures. It discusses relational, dimensional, and NoSQL modeling techniques; introduces normalization and denormalization strategies; and explores how AI, metadata, and semantic modeling are redefining enterprise data management for the next decade.
